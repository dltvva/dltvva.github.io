Tribute for Lionel Messi

About

Argentine footballer, forward and captain of MLS club Inter Miami, captain of the Argentina national team. World champion, two-time Copa America winner, Olympic champion. Eight-time winner of the Ballon d'Or and six-time winner of the Golden Boot.

Important events

  1. 1987 — born in Rosario, Argentina
  2. 2004 — debut for FC Barcelona
  3. 2009 — first Ballon d'Or and first Champions League
  4. 2022 — won the World Cup with Argentina

Key contributions

  • Revolutionized football with unique dribbling and precise passing
  • Led Barcelona to 10 La Liga titles and 4 Champions League victories
  • Set numerous records, including most goals and 7 Ballon d'Ors
